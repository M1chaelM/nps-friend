 $p$: ``Process p is in the critical section.''\\

 $p'$: ``Process p is attempting to enter the critical section.\\
 $q'$: ``Process q is attempting to enter the critical section.''\\
\begin{parts}
\part[2] Use the propositions given above to express the property of
mutual exclusion in linear temporal logic.

$\Box \ngg (p \land q)$
\end{solution}

liveness in linear temporal logic.
\vspace{15mm}

$\Box (p' \imp \Diamond p) \land \Box (q' \imp \Diamond q)$
\end{solution}
\end{parts}
